## RECORDING NOTES

### B-Roll Suggestions
- Close-up of coffee beans being poured
- Hands cupping a warm mug
- Coffee shop ambiance shots
- Roasting machine in action (if available)

### Screen Recording Tips
- Use 1920x1080, clean browser (no bookmarks bar)
- Slow, deliberate mouse movements
- Pause briefly on key features
- Consider picture-in-picture of face in corner

### Voiceover Tips
- Record in quiet room, use pop filter
- Smile while talking (it comes through)
- Conversational pace, not rushed
- Re-record any tongue-twisters

### Tools
- Screen recording: OBS, Loom, or ScreenFlow
- Editing: DaVinci Resolve (free) or Premiere
- Music: Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or royalty-free
